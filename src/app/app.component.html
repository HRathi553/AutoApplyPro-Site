How it Works

A simple but powerful workflow that completely automates your job applications while keeping your account safe.

01

Configure Profile

Enter your Gmail app credentials, upload your PDF resume, and define the specific role you are targeting.

02

Select Browser

Auto-detect or manually select your installed Chromium-based browser (Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Brave) for secure automation.

03

Provide Job Search URL

Paste a LinkedIn search URL with your desired filters (e.g., specific location, remote, experience level) targeting LinkedIn posts.

04

Automated Outreach

The app scrapes LinkedIn posts, intelligently extracts email addresses from the content, sends personalized cover letters, and respects daily safety limits (max 50/day) to prevent bans.
